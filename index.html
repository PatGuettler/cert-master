Certification practice — free, in your browser

Cert Master is an unofficial study tool for AWS, Microsoft Azure, Google Cloud, and CompTIA certifications: original practice questions aligned to official exam guides, each with links to vendor documentation. Includes domain-weighted exams, progress dashboards, and CompTIA acronym drills. No account or install required.

How to use Cert Master

  1. Pick a certification from Browse certifications in the menu or on the home page.
  2. Review exam domains and optional settings in the menu (timer, feedback on Next, doc links).
  3. Start a practice exam — each attempt draws a new random set weighted like the official exam guide.
  4. Submit and review your score, domain breakdown, study plan, flagged questions, and drill mode for weak topics.
  5. Track progress on the dashboard; export or clear your data under My data in the menu.
  6. CompTIA only: use Study acronyms on the exam page to drill abbreviations both ways before a full practice test.

Progress is saved in this browser only. Use Export backup before switching devices. Questions are original practice items aligned to official exam objectives—not copied from real certification exams.
