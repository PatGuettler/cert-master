Privacy policy

Last updated: May 22, 2026

Cert Master respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how ads work, and how to reach us. By using practicecert.com you agree to this policy.

Information we collect

No account or login is required. Exam progress, settings, and KeyTrain state are stored in your browser's localStorage on your device. We do not operate a user database or sell personal information.

Standard web server logs may be recorded by our hosting provider for security and operations. We do not use those logs to build marketing profiles.

Cookies and advertising

We may display Google AdSense ads. Google and its partners may use cookies to serve and measure ads. Learn more in How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps and manage preferences at Google Ads Settings.

We do not place ads in pop-ups, emails, or software downloads. Ads are not styled to look like navigation buttons.

Your choices

  • Clear site data via the menu “Clear data” options or your browser settings.
  • Block cookies in your browser (some features or ads may not work as expected).
  • Email patguettlerpages@gmail.com for privacy questions.

Children

Cert Master is intended for certification candidates and adult learners. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy when practices or legal requirements change. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
